Fo Guang Shan Buddha Memorial Center

Fo Guang Shan Temple, Fo Guang Shan Buddha Memorial Center (佛光山佛陀紀念館) The highlight of the temple is the giant Buddha image with 8 large pagodas lined up on both sides of the path leading to the Buddha image. magnificently beautiful Inside the building is divided into several zones. The country's largest Buddhist museum has a golden Buddha hall. Jade Buddha Hall Guanyin Bodhisattva Hall and also enshrined the relics of the Buddha's tooth

This is an old abandoned warehouse area by the water. Currently, it has been redeveloped into an arts center, including street arts, floating sculptures, exhibition halls, parks and design shops. It is one of the gathering places for people in Kaohsiung. Both teenagers and seniors

Dome of Life at Formosa Boulevard Station

The Dome of Life is a stained-glass dome located in the center of KMRT Formosa Boulevard Station. It features over 4,500 multicolored glass art pieces, making it the world's largest subway art centerpiece. It was designed by Italian artist Narcissus Quagliata. That took up to 4 years to build.

Liuhe Night Market

"Liuhe Night Market" is a night market that combines a lot of delicious food. It looks strange, new, but delicious. It is a night market that is especially suitable for tourists. Because almost every restaurant has an English menu. Importantly, it is located on Liuhe 2nd Road, next to the KMRT Formosa Boulevard Station.

Cijin Island

Beaches, fish markets, in Kaohsiung there is an island called Cijin Island (Chijing). “Seafood is delicious and there are many places to visit.” It takes only 2-4 hours to travel around the island. This is an island with history. There are many tourist attractions that should be visited such as Cihou Lighthouse (beautiful lighthouse by the sea), Cihou Fort (ancient cliff-side fortress), Wind Power Park (windmill courtyard), Cijin Star Tunnel (star tunnel), Cijin Old Street ( Fresh seafood restaurant on the side of the road)

Ruifeng Night Market

Ruifeng (pronounced Rui Feng) is the largest night market in Kaohsiung and Pingtung. Located north of the city in Tsoying district, this local-style night market has been in operation for more than 20 years.

Glass walkway

Siaogangshan Skywalk Park is one of the city's tourism construction projects in Gangshan District, located in the eastern part of the city. It is Kaohsiung's first and only Skywalk. It opened to the public on the first day of Chinese New Year on February 15, 2018. Siaogangshan Skywalk Park, shaped like a violin head, pillar height 40.5 meters, length 88 meters, width is twice the length. There are 24 strings that look like violin strings.

Cruise on the Aihe River

Ai He River Ai He (Love River) is the center of the city from south to north. In the past, this river was black. There is a very polluted smell from the industrial factory. After that, the city council invested a lot of money. To take care and clean the water in the river to be clean and clear like today.
